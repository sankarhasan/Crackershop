### Sivakasi
- Definition：Fireworks manufacturing capital of India where KPR Crackers sources its products directly; used throughout the site as a quality/trust signal ("Direct from Sivakasi").

### Supreme Fireworks
- Definition：The manufacturer brand that KPR Crackers is an authorized dealer of; referenced in the top bar and about section as the source of quality assurance.

### Minimum order ₹2,000
- Definition：Business rule enforced on the cart drawer: checkout is disabled until the cart subtotal reaches ₹2,000; displayed both as a persistent badge in the top bar and as a live warning inside the cart drawer.
- Aliases：min order ₹2000

### Combo Packs
- Definition：Product category (id 9) representing curated multi-item boxes such as "KPR Diwali Family Combo" and "KPR Grand Festive Combo" sold at discounted bundle pricing.
- Aliases：combos

### Quick Enquiry
- Definition：Customer-facing form section (`#enquiry`) where visitors submit name, WhatsApp number, delivery address, product interest and message; submissions are persisted to `localStorage.jcs_enquiries` and later viewed in the admin panel.
- Aliases：enquiry form

### WhatsApp Widget / Quick Enquiry Popup
- Definition：Floating bottom-right chat widget (`#whatsapp-widget`) that opens a mini form collecting name, phone and message, then launches a WhatsApp conversation link — the primary outbound contact channel besides phone calls.
- Aliases：wa popup、wa quick form
