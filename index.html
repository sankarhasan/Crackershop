CELEBRATE SAFELY

Essential Safety Tips

Follow these rules to ensure a colorful and safe Diwali celebration for your family and pets.

✔️

Dos

  • Always light crackers in open, clear outdoor spaces.
  • Keep a bucket of water and sand nearby for emergencies.
  • Store your crackers in a closed, dry box away from fire sources.
  • Wear well-fitting, tight cotton clothing while lighting fireworks.
  • Maintain safe distance as specified on product boxes.
❌

Don'ts

  • Never attempt to re-ignite a firecracker that has failed to light (duds).
  • Don't hold crackers in hand while lighting them.
  • Never wear loose-fitting synthetic clothes (polyester/nylon).
  • Never light firecrackers inside or near enclosed buildings.
  • Avoid throwing crackers at animals, pets, or people.
